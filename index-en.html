About Us

From Rebranding to Leadership: The ASR Story

Our company, founded in 2009 during a period of rapid development of the food industry in Azerbaijan, underwent a full rebranding process in response to the reform wave that continued until 2019.

2009
Company Founded
Operations began in response to the local consulting need for the food industry. First projects were carried out in the meat processing sector.
2013
International Certification
First Lead Auditor certificates were obtained. The international accreditation process under ISO 22000 was successfully completed.
2019
Full Rebranding & Expansion
In response to new food safety legislation, the company was rebranded and the service portfolio was expanded to cover strategic areas.
2024
Regional Leadership Position
With 50+ experts and 100+ successful projects, it became one of Azerbaijan's largest consulting groups.

Successful Project Examples

Real problems, solutions we applied, and measurable results.

Meat Processing Müəssisəsi 2023
Problem

The company was not fully meeting HACCP requirements. Critical violations in the cold chain had been detected and a warning had been issued by AQTA.

Applied Solution

A full HACCP plan was developed, a real-time monitoring system was established for 4 critical points, and staff underwent a 40-hour training program.

Result

✓ Production losses reduced by 20%. ISO 22000 certificate obtained in 8 months. AQTA inspection concluded positively.

Dairy Producer 2022
Problem

The company had no readiness for IFS certification. Export plans to the European market were blocked due to certification requirements.

Applied Solution

A gap analysis was conducted, a 6-month preparation roadmap was developed, and the document base was aligned with international standards.

Result

✓ IFS certificate obtained. Export volume increased by 35% in the first year. Agreement signed with a European distributor.

HoReCa Network 2023
Problem

The human resources structure of a restaurant network with 12 branches was inefficient. Staff turnover was high and the number of customer complaints had increased.

Applied Solution

An HR audit was conducted, job descriptions were redeveloped, a performance evaluation system was established, and management training was conducted.

Result

✓ Staff turnover reduced by 45%. Customer satisfaction increased by 28%. Operating costs optimized by 15%.
